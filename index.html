About Me

My name is Marcia Adler. I am a former consultant, looking in NYC, who is looking to make a career change into tech. I am learning to code so I can become a softtware engineer and work on projects that help bring company's visions to life.

Prior to attending the Columbia Engineering Coding Bootcamp, I worked as a consultant in the area of human capital, working on transformatons and M&A projects. I worked for a large consulting firm, as well as an internal consultant for a globla organization.

I have an MBA in Finance and Human Capital from the University of Connecticut. I also have a Bachelors in Business and Technology from UConn.

I enjoy solving problems and working with others to help organization achieve their goals. Once I graduate I would like to work for an organization, potentially a startup, helping bring their vision, and products, to life using the coding skills I have gained in the Bootcamp.

When I'm not working or studying, I have a cat at home, named Princess, who is the sweetest thing. I also love spending time with my family and friends. I also love to travel and explore new places. I have spent time living in Spain, Costa Rica and Hawaii. I have also traveled to several other states and countries for both pleasure and business. During my time as a consultant, I traveled to Germany and Canada, as well as to several different states in the US for work.
